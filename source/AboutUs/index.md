## 🎨 Artists & Animation Team

Our Artists & Animation Team plays a vital role in shaping the visual identity and player experience of our games. From concept art to in-game animation, they collaborate closely with the development team to ensure a cohesive and visually compelling product.

- 🎨 The artists are responsible for creating environments, characters, UI assets, and illustrations that define the game's look and feel.
- 🎞️ The animation team brings these visuals to life — from subtle character motions to eye-catching VFX — adding personality and polish to gameplay.

Although they work behind the scenes, their impact is front and center in every game we create.
