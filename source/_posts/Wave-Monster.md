## 👾 Wave Monster — Survive the Monster Onslaught!

**Dodge. Shoot. Upgrade. Survive.**

Welcome to **Wave Monster** — a thrilling casual survival game that challenges you to face endless waves of monsters. Test your reflexes and strategy as you dodge enemies, collect items, and power up to fight back!

---

### 📝 Overview

In Wave Monster, players step into a battlefield swarming with monsters. Your mission is simple: survive. Collect upgrades, dodge projectiles, and eliminate as many enemies as possible.

The game offers quick sessions of intense action, perfect for mobile gamers who love fast-paced challenges.

- Non-stop wave-based action gameplay
- Pickups and upgrades for weapons and abilities
- Increasing difficulty with each wave
- Unique enemies and colorful effects
- Smooth controls and satisfying feedback
