## 🐰 Bunny Harvest

**Run, Pick, Dodge — And Survive the Attacking Worms!**

**Bunny Harvest** is a lively, fast-paced **reaction game** where your adorable bunny races through colorful gardens to collect fresh radishes while dodging hostile worm shooters! Move quickly, harvest wisely, and stay alert — because the worms never stop firing.

---

### 🎮 How to Play

- 🕹️ **Move**: Use the joystick to control your bunny across the garden.
- 🌱 **Harvest**: Run into ripe radishes to collect them instantly.
- 💜 **Dodge**: Avoid purple projectiles fired by moving worm enemies — getting hit costs you hearts.
- ⏱️ **Survive**: Radishes regrow in cycles and grow faster over time. Keep up!
- 🔗 **Combo**: Chain perfect harvests for bonus rewards.

---

### 🔥 Key Features

- ⚡ **Fast Harvesting Action** — Pick ripe radishes for points while managing incoming threats.
- 🪱 **Worm Shooter Enemies** — Dodge purple projectiles fired by moving worms across the field.
- 🌿 **Dynamic Growing Field** — Radishes regrow constantly and increase in speed as the game progresses.
- 🕹️ **Simple Controls** — Smooth joystick movement, easy for all players.
- 🗺️ **Maps to Unlock** — Play in different garden layouts with unique visuals and paths.
- 🐇 **Skins to Unlock** — Collect radishes to buy fun costumes for your bunny.
- ⚡ **Quick Sessions** — Perfect for short, satisfying runs on mobile.

---

### 💊 Power-Ups

| Power-Up     | Effect                                        |
|--------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| 🧲 Magnet    | Pulls all ripe radishes toward you            |
| ⚡ Speed Boost | Sprint faster for quick harvesting           |
| 🛡️ Shield   | Protect yourself from worms — and counter-attack! |

### 📝 Overview

Built with a **cute low-poly style** and hand-drawn outline shading, Bunny Harvest brings bright, cheerful gardens filled with flowers, mushrooms, and trees. Juicy animation feedback, colorful visual effects, and a satisfying harvest loop make every run feel rewarding.

Key Highlights:
- 🎨 Cute low-poly visuals with cheerful garden environments
- 🎵 Lively sound design and juicy animation feedback
- 🏆 Endless survival with escalating difficulty
- 🐇 Cosmetic progression — new maps and skins earned through gameplay
