## 🧩 Connect Dots: Clear The All

**Connect. Clear. Conquer the Puzzle!**

**Connect Dots: Clear The All** is a satisfying and addictive puzzle game where you connect matching slimes to complete the board. With each level, new challenges, obstacles, and objectives await, requiring you to think ahead and strategize with limited moves.

---

### 🎮 How to Play

- 🎯 **Match Colors**: Draw a line connecting slimes of the same color.
- ⛓️ **Chain Strategy**: The longer the chain, the better the result.
- 🧠 **Plan Your Moves**: Clear all targets before you run out of moves.
- ⚡ **Use Power-Ups**: Unleash powerful tools to help you through tricky levels.

The simple tap-and-drag mechanic makes it instantly playable, but the deeper puzzles keep it rewarding for puzzle fans of all ages.

---

### 📝 Overview

Featuring a bright and colorful interface, **Connect Dots** is ideal for anyone seeking a blend of relaxing visuals with mentally stimulating gameplay. Whether you're in the mood for a quick play or a longer puzzle session, it delivers!

Key Features:
- 🌈 Colorful 2D puzzle design
- 🧠 Smart puzzles with limited-move objectives
- ⚙️ Power-ups and combos to boost gameplay
- 📱 Designed for portrait mobile play
- 📴 Works offline, no internet required
