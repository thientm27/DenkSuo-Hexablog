## 🦷 Doctor Dentist: Animal Teeth Care

**Be the dentist! Treat your animal patients and bring back their happy smiles.**

**Doctor Dentist** is a colorful and engaging casual game for kids, where players become a dentist for cute animals like pandas, hippos, crocodiles, and more. It’s both fun and educational — teaching children about dental hygiene through playful interaction.

---

### 🧸 Gameplay Features

- 🐼 Treat different animal patients with unique personalities and tooth problems.
- 🦷 Use tools like toothbrushes, drills, pliers, and water jets.
- 😁 Watch their expressions change as you clean, fix, and polish their teeth.
- ✨ Bright and colorful 2D cartoon graphics that appeal to young audiences.
- 🧠 Teaches kids about the importance of oral care in a fun, stress-free way.

---

### 📝 Overview

Doctor Dentist was designed with young players in mind. The UI is intuitive, the animations are expressive, and the sound effects are satisfying. It promotes gentle, relaxing play and encourages children to learn basic hygiene habits.

Highlights:
- 🎮 Simple tap-based gameplay
- 🪥 Encourages positive learning through play
- 🎨 Kid-friendly art style and sounds
- 📴 Works fully offline
