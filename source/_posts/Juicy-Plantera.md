## 🍊 Juicy Plantera — Grow, Harvest, Solve, Profit!

**Welcome to the orchard of strategy and sweetness.**
**Juicy Plantera** blends relaxing 3D farming with strategic resource management and logic-based mini-games. It's not just about planting — it’s about thriving in a vibrant and risk-filled farming world.

---

### 📝 Overview

Grow your fruit garden block by block, harvest ripe crops, and dive into logical mini-games that test both your management and your mind. But beware: one bad financial decision could bankrupt your entire garden!

| Feature        | Detail                        |
|----------------|-------------------------------|
| Genre          | 3D Farming Simulator + Puzzle |
| View           | Top-down, rotatable camera    |
| Platforms      | iOS & Android                 |
| Style          | Cartoonish, colorful 3D       |
| Audience       | Casual (10+)                  |

---

### 🌱 Farming Core Loop

- **Start Small**: Begin with a 3×3 land grid (only 1 tile unlocked).
- **Plant & Grow**: Use seeds and fertilizer to grow crops like oranges, apples, and durians.
- **Harvest & Replant**: When the progress slider is full, harvest and replant for continuous yield.
- **Expand**: Unlock more tiles and manage resources wisely.

### 🧮 Logic-Based Business Mini-Games

#### 🛒 Mini-Game 1: Retail Shop Weighing

- Objective: Match orders using correct fruit sizes and weights.
- Gameplay:
    - Drag fruits to the scale to match order conditions like:
        - “2 Fruits — Total 800g”
        - “Include at least 1 size XS”
    - Sizes: XS (100g), S (200g), M (300g), L (400g), XL (500g)
- Scoring:
    - Correct → Earn coins
    - Incorrect → Lose selected fruits

#### 🏭 Mini-Game 2: Packing Factory

- Objective: Sort and pack fruits into the correct boxes on time.
- Gameplay:
    - Conveyor belt moves fruits horizontally
    - Drag correct fruits into matching cartons
    - Each carton displays:
        - Fruit type, timer, progress bar, required weight
- Scoring:
    - Success → Full coin reward
    - Fail → Lose value or get no coins
