## 🏓 Bouncing Ball

**Classic Pong. Reimagined. In the Future.**

**Bouncing Ball** is a fast-paced **3D arcade game** set in a neon-lit underground future city. Modified delivery drones compete in chaotic arena matches, using holographic shields to bounce powerful energy balls. Simple concept, insane execution.

---

### 🎮 How to Play

- ↔️ **Move Your Drone**: Use left and right buttons to position your drone and defend your goal.
- ⚡ **Bounce the Energy Ball**: Block incoming shots and redirect the ball toward enemy goals.
- 🚀 **Survive the Escalation**: Each collision makes the ball move faster — the longer the rally, the more intense it gets.
- 🏆 **Score to Win**: First drone to reach the required score wins. If time runs out, the highest score takes it.

---

### 🔥 Key Features

- 🏓 **Classic Pong Reimagined in 3D** — Experience legendary bounce gameplay in a vibrant futuristic drone arena.
- ⚡ **Dynamic Energy Ball Physics** — Balls accelerate after every hit, creating intense and unpredictable rallies.
- 🤖 **3 Competitive Game Modes** — Face increasing pressure with more drones and higher score requirements.
- 🎨 **Color-Changing Ball System** — Each hit changes the ball color, making it easy to track scoring ownership.
- 🎮 **Arcade Consumables** — Slow time with Energy Drinks or hijack all balls using Beacon tech.
- 📈 **Drone Progression System** — Upgrade movement speed and shield range to dominate tougher matches.
- 💰 **Reward-Based Matches** — Win games to earn coins and unlock stronger performance tools.

---

### 📝 Overview

A central energy cannon fires glowing balls in unpredictable directions. Your mission: defend your goal and redirect the ball to score against your opponents. With multiple drones in the arena and unlimited shots from the cannon, every match quickly turns into high-speed chaos.

Key Highlights:
- 🌌 Stylish neon sci-fi arenas with glowing environments and holographic effects
- 💥 Chaotic multi-drone battles — more opponents mean more balls, more speed, more unpredictability
- 🎯 Skill-based positioning — perfect timing and movement are the keys to survival
- 🏅 Short matches make it ideal for quick sessions or leaderboard grinding
