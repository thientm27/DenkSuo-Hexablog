## 🍉 Fruit Merge: Make Giant Fruit

**Drop, Merge, and Reach the Giant Watermelon!**

**Merge Fruit** is a satisfying puzzle game inspired by the viral "Watermelon Game." Your goal is simple: drop fruits into the bowl, match two of the same kind, and merge them into a bigger one. Keep going until you get the legendary watermelon!

---

### 🎮 How to Play

- 🍎 **Drag & Drop**: Slide your finger to position the fruit.
- 🍊 **Match & Merge**: Combine two same fruits to form the next-level fruit.
- 🍉 **Watch the Chaos**: The bowl gets crowded fast — plan your merges carefully!
- 💣 **Game Over**: When the fruits overflow the top of the bowl, it's game over!

The physics-based movement and satisfying merge effect create an addictive experience that keeps players coming back.

---

### 📝 Overview

The game features a vibrant, cute 2D art style with juicy fruit designs. It’s lightweight, fully offline, and optimized for short casual play sessions.

Key Features:
- 😄 Super easy to play
- 🧠 Addictive and strategic as you go
- 🍇 Supports leaderboard and local high scores
- 🎨 Delightful art and bounce physics
- 📴 Works offline – perfect for travel or downtime
