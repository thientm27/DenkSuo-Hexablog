## 🧠 Neuron Defense — Tower Defense in the Mind

**Protect your core, defend the brain.**  
Enter the microscopic battlefield of the mind in **Neuron Defense**, a strategic tower defense game where you connect neuron towers to repel invading mental viruses like Anxiety, Depression, and Obsession.

---

### 📝 Overview

Build and link neuron towers to create a flexible defense network inside the brain. Draw neural pathways, place powerful neuron types, and defend your **Core Neuron** against invading threats. The deeper your network, the more powerful your defense — but enemies get smarter with every wave.

---

### 🧠 Gameplay Highlights

- 🧬 **Freeform Neural Linking**: Create flexible network paths by connecting neurons freely across the grid.
- 🧱 **Dynamic Enemy Movement**: Enemies follow the paths you create — giving you both freedom and responsibility.
- 🧘 **Mental Defense Towers**:
    - **Logic**: Rapid-fire attackers.
    - **Meditation**: Heal connected neurons.
    - **Memory**: Slows down mind viruses.
    - **Intuition**: Deals splash damage.
- ⚠️ **Mental Virus Enemies**: Each type affects your neurons differently — some are fast, some hit hard, some explode!

---

### 🧠 Core Gameplay Mechanics

- **Draw to Connect**: Link new neurons directly or indirectly to the Core Neuron.
- **Energy Economy**: Earn energy by defeating enemies and spend it to expand your neural defense.
- **Tower Placement**: Drag & drop neuron types onto drawn links.
- **Win/Lose Conditions**:
    - ✅ Win: Keep the Core Neuron alive through all waves.
    - ❌ Lose: Core Neuron runs out of health.

|---------------------|-------------------------------|-------|------|--------------|

| Stress              | Tanky but slow                 | 4.0   | 700  | 15           |

| Emotional Damage 💀 | Boss, spawns small enemies     | 6.5   | 1500 | 50           |

> *Depression*: Deals 20 damage to its target and 10 to linked neurons.

---

### 🧩 Level Design & Progression

- **Campaign Mode**: Levels include 4–10 waves with increasing difficulty.
- **Boss Levels**: Feature powerful mental viruses like "Emotional Damage".
- **Star Ratings**:
    - ⭐⭐⭐: Core takes no damage.
    - ⭐⭐: Core remains above 50% HP.
    - ⭐: Core survives with any HP left.
- **Rewards**:  
  Coins = (`Level + 50`) × star rate  
  (100% for 3⭐, 50% for 2⭐, 25% for 1⭐)

---

### 🛒 Shop & Upgrade System

- **Unlock Neurons**: Spend coins to unlock advanced neuron types.
- **Upgrade Levels**: Each neuron has up to 5 levels.
