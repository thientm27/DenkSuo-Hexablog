## 👣 Echo Step

**Every Step Echoes. Every Move Matters.**

**Echo Step** is a mysterious **3D puzzle adventure** set across the Treasure Islands. You are a brave explorer stranded on magical islands filled with puzzles, hidden treasures, and dangerous demons. The twist? When your steps run out, a shadow demon is summoned — and it replays your every move. Master this mechanic, and it becomes your most powerful weapon.

---

### 🎮 How to Play

- 🧭 **Plan Your Route**: Each island is a logic puzzle. Think before you move.
- 👣 **Limited Steps**: Every move you make counts toward your step budget.
- 💎 **Reach the Treasure**: Navigate to the treasure chest tile to collect your reward.
- 👹 **Shadow Demon Spawns**: When steps run out, a demon appears and mimics your past movements exactly — use this to your advantage to solve puzzles and block paths.

---

### 🔥 Key Features

- 🏝️ **Unique Puzzle Islands** — Each island presents a new challenge that tests your strategy and spatial thinking.
- 👣 **Limited Step Gameplay** — Every move counts. Use your steps wisely or the demon gets you.
- 👹 **Shadow Demon Mechanic** — A demon that echoes your movements is both the threat and the solution. Turn danger into strategy.
- 💎 **Hidden Treasures** — Reach treasure chest tiles to collect precious rewards across islands.
- 🧠 **Smart Strategy Puzzles** — Plan ahead, predict the demon's path, and solve each layout cleverly.
- 🌍 **Adventure Through Many Islands** — Discover new challenges across a growing archipelago of mysteries.

---

### 📝 Overview

Echo Step's core innovation is the **shadow demon mechanic** — a system where your past movement becomes an active element of the puzzle. Knowing when to spend steps, when to lure the demon, and when to use its echo as a decoy is what separates good players from great ones.

Key Highlights:
- 🎯 Easy to learn, deeply strategic to master
- 🌴 Charming island visual style with mysterious atmosphere
- 🧩 Puzzle design that rewards lateral thinking
- 🔄 Each island is a self-contained challenge — no grinding, pure puzzle
