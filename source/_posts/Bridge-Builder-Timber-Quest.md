## 🌉 Bridge Builder: Timber Quest — Strategic Puzzle Adventure

**Build smarter, not harder.**
Step into the world of **Bridge Builder: Timber Quest**, a strategic puzzle game where every move counts. Collect timber, plan your route, and construct bridges to reach the goal — but beware, there’s no turning back!

---

### 📝 Overview

In this brain-challenging adventure, players swipe to guide their character across floating blocks, collecting scattered timber to build bridges and bypass obstacles. A wrong turn or missing a piece could leave you stranded!

---

### 🧠 Gameplay Highlights

- 🪵 **Collect & Construct**: Gather wooden pieces to build essential bridges over gaps and obstacles.
- 🧩 **Strategic Swiping**: Each swipe moves the player forward — no turning back, so plan carefully!
- ❌ **Obstacles & Traps**: From rocks and trees to tricky traps, each level tests your foresight.
- 🧠 **Progressive Difficulty**: New challenges and mechanics appear as levels advance.

---

### 🎮 Core Mechanics

- **Swipe to Move**: Move across tiles in 3 directions — left, right, or forward.
- **Timber Gathering**: Only blocks with timber count — miss one and the bridge might not build!
- **Obstacle Interaction**:
    - Rocks: Block movement completely.
    - Trees: Delay movement by 1 second.
    - Traps: Remove collected timber or send the player back.

---

### 🌲 Level Design & Progression

| Level | Required Wood | Notable Features                    |
|-------|----------------|-------------------------------------|
| 1     | 3 logs         | Intro with basic movement           |
| 5     | 6 logs         | Introduces simple traps             |
| 10    | 8 logs         | Complex wood placement, backtrack   |
| 20    | 12 logs        | High-density traps, maze structure  |

---

### 🧱 Obstacle Types

| Type      | Effect                               |
|-----------|--------------------------------------|
| Rock      | Completely blocks the player’s path  |
| Tree      | Causes a delay before movement       |
| Trap      | Deducts timber or resets position    |
