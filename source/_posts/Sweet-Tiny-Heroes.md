## 🐜 Sweet Tiny Heroes

**Think Smart. Solve Puzzles. Guide the Tiny Ant Hero!**

**Sweet Tiny Heroes** is a charming **3D puzzle-adventure game** where you control a tiny but clever ant on a journey through a dangerous and vibrant forest. Each level is a logic-based puzzle — observe the environment carefully, plan your moves, and interact with objects in the correct order to reach your goal.

---

### 🎮 How to Play

- 🧱 **Push Soil Blocks** into pits to build ant nests and create bridges.
- 🌊 **Fill Rivers** with blocks to cross water obstacles.
- 💣 **Activate Bombs** to destroy walls and clear blocked paths.
- 🕷️ **Avoid Enemies** — three types of deadly spiders patrol the forest at all times.
- 🃏 **Use Skill Cards** to overcome tougher challenges when brute logic isn't enough.

---

### 🕷️ Enemy Types

| Spider       | Behavior                                      |
|--------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| ⚫ Black      | Patrols key areas, blocks your path           |
| 🟢 Green     | Shoots poisonous projectiles at the player    |
| 🔴 Red       | Leaves fire trails on the ground              |

---

### 🃏 Skill Cards

Collect Gems and Coins throughout levels to unlock powerful Skill Cards:

| Skill Card          | Effect                                          |
|---------------------|-------------------------------------------------|
| ⚡ Speed Boost       | Move faster for a short time                   |
| ❄️ Freeze Spiders   | Temporarily freeze all spiders to move safely  |
| 🛡️ Protective Shield | Block incoming damage from enemies            |
| 🌊 Walk on Water    | Cross rivers without filling them with blocks  |

---

### 🔥 Key Features

- 🧩 **Grid-Based Puzzle Gameplay** — Easy to learn, genuinely hard to master.
- 🌿 **Smart Environment Interaction** — Soil blocks, water, bombs, fire, and three spider types all interact with each other.
- 🐜 **Charming Ant Hero** — Colorful, polished 3D visuals with a cute protagonist.
- 🎯 **Multiple Solutions** — Combine skill cards with environment manipulation for creative approaches.
- 😌 **Relaxing Pace** — No timers, no rush — just satisfying problem-solving.

---

### 📝 Overview

Sweet Tiny Heroes proves that even the smallest hero can overcome big challenges — with brains, timing, and creativity. The game rewards players who observe carefully and plan methodically, while Skill Cards add flexibility for those who want to experiment.

Key Highlights:
- 🎨 Cute, polished 3D art style with vibrant forest environments
- 🧠 Puzzle design built around layered environment interaction
- 🃏 Skill system adds replayability and creative problem solving
- 🐜 A wholesome story of a tiny ant taking on a very big forest
