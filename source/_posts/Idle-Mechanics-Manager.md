## 🔧 Idle Mechanics Manager — Rule the Garage!

**Manage. Upgrade. Earn.**  
Become the ultimate repair shop tycoon in this fun and relaxing idle strategy game.

---

### 📝 Overview

In **Idle Mechanics Manager**, you’re the boss of a growing auto repair business. Hire skilled workers, fix vehicles, expand your garage, and rake in the profits — even when you’re offline. The game combines idle mechanics with satisfying management gameplay, making it perfect for casual players who love progress and strategy.

- Repair and upgrade vehicles
- Expand your shop operations
- Unlock powerful upgrades and boosts
- Earn while idle and automate your workflow
- Vibrant 2D visuals in landscape mode
- Web & Mobile (Telegram-ready)
