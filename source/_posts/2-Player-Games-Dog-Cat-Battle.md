## 🐾 Paws & Claws — The Ultimate 2-Player Rivalry!

**2 players. 1 device. Endless fun. One timeless rivalry…**
Get ready to dive into the classic showdown between cats and dogs in **Paws & Claws** — a colorful, cartoon-style collection of casual **PvP mini-games** perfect for players of all ages!

---

### 📝 Overview

Battle it out in nearly **20 unique challenges** that test your reflexes, strategy, and quick thinking — all on a **single device**, completely **offline** and **free to play**.
Whether you're looking to spark some friendly rivalry between friends, siblings, or couples, these lighthearted games bring fast-paced action and laugh-out-loud moments.

---

### 🧩 Game Highlights

- 🐶 **Pate Bombers**: Dinner is on the line! Drop bombs and outsmart your opponent to win the feast.
- 🧙 **Demon Slayers**: Slash away evil spirits and claim your title as the ultimate exorcist.
- 🏴‍☠️ **Sea of Thieves**: Set sail, collect treasure, and rule the seas in a pirate-themed face-off!
- 🧟 **Pet Pandemic**: Zombies are coming — who can survive the longest in this end-of-the-world brawl?
- 🐹 **Ham Hoppers**: Hop your way through the wasteland and fight for scraps!
- 🌌 **Star Collectors**: Blast off and grab the shiniest stars in the galaxy.
...and many more surprises waiting for you!
