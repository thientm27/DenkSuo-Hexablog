# 🐾 Animal Rising: Fighting human

A Play-to-Earn tower defense game where animals rise up to protect their forest from ruthless pirates. Built on the **Solana** ecosystem, Animal Defenders delivers real-time strategy gameplay combined with NFT elements and blockchain login integration.

---

## 🔥 Game Overview

The game tells the story of a battle between various animal species fighting against pirates who are attacking and destroying the forest. Players command a team of animals, deploying them strategically to defend their territory.

- 🐵 Recruit unique animal heroes
- 🪓 Fight against waves of pirate invaders
- 🌳 Upgrade tree health and production
- 💎 Earn rewards and progress through waves
---

## 🧠 Key Features

### 🔐 Solana Wallet Login
- Blockchain login via **Solana wallet**
- Data is securely managed through **PlayFab** (Microsoft backend)

### 📊 Upgrade System
Players can upgrade various attributes using in-game resources:

#### Animal Upgrades
- Unlock and level up animals with special skills
- Example: **Backattack Gator** – sneaks behind enemies and cripples them
- Stats: ❤️ 35 HP | 🗡️ 5 DMG

![Upgrade Menu](https://telegraph-image-denkstudio.pages.dev/api/cfile/AgACAgUAAyEGAASLAAFR7QADKWhRX627mmm5l9NzlHzk5mHv8vG1AAKAxDEbA5-IVgyf8JqIognlAQADAgADdwADNgQ)

#### Stage & Resource Upgrades
- Increase food production rate and storage
- Boost health for trees and the main home tree

![Upgrade Shop](https://telegraph-image-denkstudio.pages.dev/api/cfile/AgACAgUAAyEGAASLAAFR7QADLmhRX7t-jt6ecS89-Mg3AxVakUr1AAKFxDEbA5-IVmL2S0diwao2AQADAgADdwADNgQ)

## 🎮 Gameplay

- Tap to deploy animals
- Hold to advance forward
- Manage food production and cooldowns
- Each enemy wave becomes increasingly difficult
