## 📦 Organize Master: Sorting Time

**Sort, relax, and enjoy the satisfaction of perfect organization!**

Welcome to **Organize Master: Sorting Time** — a soothing and creative casual game that challenges your inner organizer. With simple drag-and-drop mechanics and a delightful art style, this game invites players to tidy up cluttered chaos into order.

---

### 📝 Overview

In each level, you're presented with a messy collection of items. Your goal? Put everything in its rightful place! As the levels advance, more types of items and higher complexity are introduced, testing your logic and problem-solving abilities.

- Visually satisfying item sorting
- Levels range from easy to brain-teasing
- Relaxing sound design and intuitive controls
- Designed for landscape mobile play

Perfect for winding down after a long day, improving focus, and sharpening your organizational skills.
