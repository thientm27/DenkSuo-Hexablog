## 🧠 AnnoyPuzzle — Looks Easy. Isn’t.

**Drag. Drop. Regret your decisions. Repeat.**

Welcome to **AnnoyPuzzle**, a deceptively simple puzzle game where every level is designed to make you think:
> “There’s no way it’s this hard… right?”

Yeah… it is.

---

### 📝 Overview

**AnnoyPuzzle** is a **drag-and-drop puzzle game** that challenges your logic, creativity, and patience.

Each level presents a small problem.  
Your job? Solve it using simple interactions.

Your reality?  
Overthinking everything and still getting it wrong.

---

### 🎮 Gameplay

- 👉 Drag objects into the correct position
- 👉 Combine elements to unlock solutions
- 👉 Experiment, fail, retry… a lot

💡 Not every solution is obvious.  
Sometimes you’ll need to:
- think differently
- try the “wrong” move first
- or question basic logic itself

---

### 🔥 Game Highlights

- 🧠 **Brain-teasing puzzles** — simple idea, tricky execution
- 🎯 **Easy controls** — just drag & drop
- 😈 **Light trolling mechanics** — the game *will* mess with you
- 📈 **Progressive difficulty** — it escalates quickly
- 🎨 **Clean & minimal design** — no distractions, just pain
