## ⚔️ Pocket Gladiators

**Fight. Upgrade. Earn.**

**Pocket Gladiators** is a **2D PvE fighting game** where players battle NPC opponents in intense 1vs1 matches. Each victory rewards gold, gems, and experience points — all used to gacha weapons, level up, and unlock character customizations.

This is a game that blends action with light RPG mechanics, enhanced with Web3 features for a Play to Earn experience.

---

### 🎮 Gameplay

- 🥊 **1v1 battles vs. AI**: Fast-paced duels that test reflexes and timing.
- 🏆 **Victory rewards**: Earn gold, gems, and XP after each win.
- 🎲 **Gacha system**: Use in-game currencies to roll for powerful weapons.
- 🎨 **Customization**: Change your character’s gear and appearance.
- 🔁 **Progression**: Gain XP to level up and unlock higher difficulty fights.
- 🎁 **Daily login bonus**: Come back daily for rewards and boosts.

The game’s level-based structure offers a casual but rewarding challenge curve, ideal for both quick sessions and long-term play.

---

### 🌐 Web3 Integration

- 🪙 Built on **Solana Ecosystem**
- 🧩 Login via **Solana Wallet**
- 📦 Backend powered by **Playfab (Microsoft)**
- 🪄 Token rewards & leaderboard potential
- 📊 On-chain progression tracking (optional)

The blockchain layer supports future reward systems and NFTs while keeping core gameplay accessible.

---

### 📝 Overview

With tight 2D fighting mechanics, gacha progression, and full Web3 support, **Pocket Gladiators** stands at the intersection of **arcade brawler** and **crypto-enabled reward systems**.

Key Highlights:
- 🎮 Skill-based combat with AI
- 🎰 Gacha + leveling mechanics
- 👕 Character gear & skin customization
- 🌍 WebGL support for cross-platform access
- 🪙 Play to Earn-ready with token integration
- 🏅 Leaderboards and online stat tracking via Playfab
