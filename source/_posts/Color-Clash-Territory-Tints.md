## 🎨 Color Clash: Territory Tints — Paint the Arena, Own the Land

**Run, paint, and dominate!**  
In **Color Clash**, players control vibrant characters racing to paint as much territory as possible in 90 seconds. Compete with bots, snatch boosters, and steal enemy colors in this chaotic color war.

---

### 🧠 Game Concept

- **Type**: Casual 3D, Territory Control
- **Mode**: Single Player (Offline with Bots)
- **Platform**: iOS, Android
- **Match Duration**: 90 seconds
- **Victory Condition**: Highest % of painted ground

---

### 🎮 Core Gameplay

#### ⚔ Match Structure

- Square arena (AxA)
- 4 total players (1 human + 3 bots)
- Paint by moving → auto-color trail
- You can **overwrite enemy color**

#### ⚡ Boosters (Pickups on Map)

| Booster       | Effect                                          | Usage     |
|---------------|--------------------------------------------------|-----------|
| Speed Boost   | x2 movement speed                                | 10 sec    |
| Paint Bomb    | Throw to instantly paint an area                 | 1 use     |
| Stun Object   | Throw to stun an enemy for 10 seconds            | 1 use     |

- Fixed number of boosters per map
- New booster spawns after one is picked

---

### 🕹️ Controls

- **Joystick (left)**: Move
- **Action Button (right)**: Use booster
- HUD shows live **% area owned per color**

---

### 🧍 Characters

- 4 main colors: 🔴 Red, 🔵 Blue, 🟡 Yellow, 🟣 Purple
- **Stats per character**:
    - Movement Speed
    - Paint Width

---

### 🗺 Map Designs

| Map Name   | Feature Description                              | Unlock Price |
|------------|--------------------------------------------------|--------------|
| Basic      | Simple, few obstacles                            | Default      |
| Wild West  | Blockers, increased booster spawn rate           | 100 coins    |
| Ice Land   | Slippery surface, all players x2 speed           | 200 coins    |
| Dungeon    | Boosters enhanced (paint bomb wider, faster)     | 400 coins    |

---

### 🧰 Items & Upgrades

| Item Type | Effect                      | Price Range |
|-----------|-----------------------------|-------------|
| Roller    | Increases paint width       | 100 – 500   |
| Trail     | Boosts movement speed       | 100 – 500   |

- Equip max 1 item per type
- Items unlocked via coins after matches

---

### 🤖 Bot AI Levels

| Difficulty | Behavior Description                                     |
|------------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| Easy       | Random movement, ignores boosters                        |
| Medium     | Prioritizes unpainted areas, ignores boosters            |
| Hard       | Prioritizes both unpainted areas and booster pickups     |

- A match includes:
    - 1 Easy Bot
    - 1 Medium Bot
    - 1 Hard Bot

---

### 💰 Game Currency

- Earn **Coins** after each match
- Coin formula:
    - Coins = % painted * tier multiplier

| Rank | Multiplier |
|------|------------|
| 1st  | 100%       |
| 2nd  | 75%        |
| 3rd  | 50%        |
| 4th  | 25%        |

---

### 📱 UI/UX

- 🎮 **Joystick** (left), **Booster Button** (right)
- 📊 **Top of screen**: Live color % bars per player
- 🛒 **Shop Menu**:
- Tabs: Maps / Items
- ✨ **Visual FX**:
- Color spreading uses animated shader
- Boosters glow for visibility

---

### 🎨 Art Style

- Low-poly, bright and clean
- Clear contrast between colored zones
- 4 main colors for characters
- Unique map theme assets:
- Forest (trees), Ice (slopes), Dungeon (bricks)
