## 🏴‍☠️ Pirate Run: Island Trader

**Sail. Loot. Deliver. Survive.**

**Pirate Run: Island Trader** is a **3D casual action game** where you take the helm of your own pirate ship and plunder the open seas. Collect as many treasure chests as you can carry, dodge enemy waves and deadly hazards, then race back to deliver your haul before your ship goes under. One wrong move and you lose it all.

---

### 🎮 How to Play

- 🕹️ **Steer Your Ship**: Use the on-screen joystick to navigate across the open ocean map.
- 📦 **Collect Chests**: Sail over scattered treasure chests to pick them up. Your ship has a carry limit — plan your route wisely.
- 🏠 **Deliver Your Loot**: Bring collected chests back to the delivery house to cash in and earn gold & gems.
- ⚔️ **Survive the Dangers**: Enemy ships spawn in escalating waves, mines drift across the sea, and tornadoes roam and scatter your entire cargo.
- 📈 **Upgrade and Keep Going**: Earn gold to upgrade your boat's speed, capacity, and gold bonus. The longer you survive, the harder it gets.

---

### ✨ Key Features

- 🚢 **Multiple Boats to Unlock** — Upgrade from a humble sloop to a powerful vessel with higher speed, more cargo capacity, and better durability.
- 🗺️ **Dynamic Treasure Spawning** — Chests constantly respawn across the map in a smart grid system, keeping the seas full of opportunity.
- 💣 **Mines & Enemy Waves** — Up to 15 mines drift across the ocean while enemy ships spawn in escalating waves, each announced before it hits.
- 🌪️ **Tornadoes** — Roaming tornadoes scatter your entire cargo instantly — the most devastating hazard on the sea.
- 🏝️ **Island-Aware Map** — Treasure never spawns on land. The world feels alive and fair.
- 🏆 **Endless High Score Mode** — No timer. Your score is the number of chests successfully delivered. Beat your personal best every run.
- 💰 **Gold Bonus System** — Invest in permanent upgrades between runs to multiply your earnings and push further each time.

---

### 📝 Overview

Pirate Run blends the tension of a resource-management game with the reflexes of an arcade runner. Every voyage is a risk-reward calculation: push deeper for more chests, or head back before the next wave hits? The escalating difficulty ensures no two runs feel the same.

Key Highlights:
- 🎯 Risk-vs-reward decision making at its core
- 🌊 Dynamic ocean with living hazards — mines, enemies, tornadoes
- 🚢 Permanent progression through ship upgrades
- ♾️ Endless replayability with personal best chasing
