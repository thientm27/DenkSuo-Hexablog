## 🐲 Dragon Dunk

**Addictive. On-chain. Earn as you play.**

**Dragon Dunk** is one of the most addictive **Play to Earn (P2E)** dragon games built on the **Solana blockchain**. With simple but challenging gameplay, players guide a little dragon through circular obstacles to score points and earn rewards — all integrated seamlessly with Web3 systems.

---

### 🎮 Gameplay

- 🔁 **Endless dunk loop**: Control the dragon to pass through rotating rings.
- ⚡ **Timing & precision**: Points are scored only when you pass at the perfect angle.
- 🎁 **Earn while playing**: In-game score links to earning mechanics on-chain.
- 🔐 **Login with Solana wallet**: Secure and seamless Web3 authentication.

Designed to be easy to pick up but hard to master, the game is suitable for both casual gamers and crypto-savvy players.

---

### 🌐 Web3 Integration

- 🪙 Built on **Solana Ecosystem**
- 🧩 Login via **Phantom Wallet**
- 📦 Backend powered by **Playfab (Microsoft)**
- 🔗 On-chain player data and leaderboards (optional)

Blockchain elements are seamlessly merged with traditional game logic, ensuring both performance and decentralization.

---

### 📝 Overview

Whether you're a fan of dragons, casual skill games, or on-chain experiences, **Dragon Dunk** brings it all together in a clean 2D package.

Key Highlights:
- 🎮 Classic arcade gameplay with a Web3 twist
- 🧠 Reaction-based challenge for all ages
- 🌍 Cross-platform WebGL build
- 📊 Online player tracking and earning history via Playfab
- 🪄 Potential NFT/Token reward system (configurable)
