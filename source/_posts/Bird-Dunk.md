## 🐦 Bird Dunk — Fun & Addictive Dunking!

**1 player. Tap to fly. Endless fun.**

Welcome to **Bird Dunk** — a charming, cartoon-style dunking game where players control a bird to aim and score through hoops. Perfect for quick casual play sessions!

---

### 📝 Overview

Take control of an adorable bird and finesse your shots through a series of hoops. With intuitive tap-and-release mechanics, score as many baskets as possible in a row to build combos and break your personal best.

- Fast-paced and addictive gameplay
- Cute and colorful 2D visuals
- Easy to learn, hard to master!
