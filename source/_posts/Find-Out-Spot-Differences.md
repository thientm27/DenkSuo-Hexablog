## 🕵️‍♂️ Find Out: Spot Differences

**Spot the difference. Train your brain. Relax and enjoy.**

**Find Out: Spot Differences** is a casual **brain-training puzzle game** with three flexible gameplay modes, each offering a different way to challenge your observation and memory.

---

### 🧩 Game Modes

This game features **3 distinct modes**, fully modular and easy to expand:

- 🔍 **Find the Difference**: Compare two images and spot the subtle changes.
- 🕵️ **Hidden Object**: Search and tap to find specific items hidden in cluttered scenes.
- 🧠 **Picture Puzzle**: Reconstruct shuffled images by sliding or tapping pieces.

All modes are configurable using **prefabs** and **ScriptableObjects**, allowing easy level management and content updates without modifying the game logic.

---

### 📝 Overview

Each level features everyday scenes—such as stationery on desks, cozy bedrooms, food trays, and pets—designed in a charming 2D cartoon style. Puzzles are timed, but stress-free, ideal for players of all ages.

Benefits:
- 🧠 Boosts concentration and memory
- ⏱️ Tests visual scanning speed
- 🎮 Supports short and casual play sessions
- 📴 Fully offline – perfect for commuting or breaks
